# overview

## Summary

This wiki is the maintained knowledge layer between immutable raw sources and downstream questions, analyses, and syntheses.

## Current synthesis

- The markdown wiki under `wiki/` is maintained by an LLM agent.
- The schema under `schema/` defines how ingestion, updating, querying, and linting work.
